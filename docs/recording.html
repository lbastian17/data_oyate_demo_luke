Thanks for working with us! Please fill in the following information in order to get your first phrase. Once you do, you will then be able to make recordings and play them back to ensure that you are satisfied with your pronounciation. It is alright if there is a little backround noise - that actually makes our model even stronger! If you are satisfied, delete all recordings except the one you want to submit and then press submit. Note: It is best to only do recordings for no more than 15-20 minutes at a time. Clarity of pronounciation may decrease for those who record for too long so be sure to take breaks!!
